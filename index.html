The Ark Process

From Assessment to Elite Performance

1

Book Assessment

Kick things off by booking an initial assessment. This helps us understand your dog's current level, temperament, and your specific requirements.

2

Consultation

We connect via Call or WhatsApp for an initial consultation. We'll discuss history and expectations to decide if our holistic approach is the right fit.

3

In-Person Interaction

We meet at your house or on the field. I interact directly with both you and your dog to observe mechanics, drive, and social dynamics in a real-world environment.

4

Bespoke Program

Based on our interaction, I develop a bespoke training curriculum tailored specifically to your dog's biology and your lifestyle goals.
